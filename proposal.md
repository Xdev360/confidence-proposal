## The Opportunity

Confidence Cargo has built a strong reputation over 23 years in freight forwarding. However, the way serious importers now find, compare, and work with forwarders has changed.

Today’s clients expect:
- Faster responses
- Instant access to pricing
- The ability to track their shipments without calling
- Professional digital experiences

Companies that fail to meet these expectations are quietly losing clients to more responsive competitors — even when their actual operations are stronger.

This proposal outlines a practical way for Confidence Cargo to close that gap and operate more efficiently.

---

## Current Challenges

After reviewing your current digital presence, here are the key areas limiting growth:

- **No instant pricing option** — Clients must fill forms and wait for responses.
- **High volume of manual enquiries** — Your team spends significant time answering repetitive questions about shipment status.
- **No self-service tracking** — Clients have no way to check cargo status independently.
- **Weak digital credibility** — Your 23 years of experience and IATA certification are not strongly communicated online.

These issues create friction in the client journey and put Confidence Cargo at a disadvantage.
